Our Story - How CatBooks Began

It all started in 2015, when a group of fantasy-loving bookworms decided to create a place where readers could find the best fantasy books in one spot. What began as a small online book club quickly grew into a thriving bookstore, fueled by a shared passion for adventure, storytelling, and magical worlds.

Since then, CatBooks has become a trusted destination for fantasy enthusiasts worldwide, offering a carefully curated selection of books, from timeless classics to exciting new releases. Today, we continue to grow, welcoming readers and creators to join our journey into the extraordinary.
